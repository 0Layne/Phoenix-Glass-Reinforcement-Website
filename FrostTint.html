Front/Tint/DecalInstallation

At ShieldX, we take pride in offering top-tier services that enhance security, aesthetics, and functionality for residential, commercial, and vehicular spaces. Our commitment to excellence ensures that each installation is executed with precision, using high-quality materials and innovative techniques to provide long-lasting results. Below is an in-depth look at our services, installation processes, and additional benefits.

Tint

Our service offers unparalleled sun-blocking capabilities. By reducing glare and harmful UV rays, our tint solutions create a more comfortable and energy-efficient environment. When combined with our security film package, you eliminate the need for traditional screens that deteriorate over time. This innovative combination not only enhances your windows' visual appeal but also provides added protection against break-ins and extreme weather conditions.

Frost

Adds a layer of elegance and privacy to your windows without compromising natural light. Perfect for spaces like bathrooms, meeting rooms, and office partitions, frosted glass offers a stylish way to obscure visibility while maintaining an open and airy feel. Whether for residential or commercial purposes, our frost films are designed to last, offering easy maintenance and a professional finish.

Tint/Frost installation process

It begins with a thorough consultation to determine the most suitable type of tint or frost film for your needs. Once selected, our trained technicians clean and prep the surface to ensure optimal adhesion. The film is then carefully applied, using precision tools to eliminate bubbles and wrinkles. The result is a flawless, durable finish that enhances both form and function.

Decal

Our Decal Installation service is ideal for businesses and individuals looking to elevate their branding or decorative efforts. Whether it's applying logos to storefront windows, branding vehicles, or creating eye-catching advertisements, we specialize in delivering smooth, bubble-free results.

Each decal is carefully aligned and applied using state-of-the-art tools to ensure precise placement and a polished appearance. This service is particularly beneficial for saving time and ensuring your decals look professional and remain durable against environmental elements.

Decal installation process

The installation process for decals begins with a design consultation to understand your vision and specific requirements. Once the decals are prepared, our team meticulously cleans the application surface to remove dust and debris. Using advanced squeegeeing and heat-setting techniques, the decals are applied seamlessly, creating a vibrant and professional finish that lasts.
