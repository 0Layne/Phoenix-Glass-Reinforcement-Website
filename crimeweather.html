Why Security Matters in High-Crime Areas

Crime rates vary across the U.S., with certain states and cities experiencing significantly higher burglary, vandalism, and property crime incidents. In high-crime areas, reinforcing vulnerable entry points can be the difference between security and intrusion.

Top 10 High-Crime States & Cities

  • 🔹 **St. Louis, MO** – One of the highest burglary and violent crime rates.
  • 🔹 **Detroit, MI** – Frequent break-ins and home invasions.
  • 🔹 **Baltimore, MD** – High rates of vandalism and theft.
  • 🔹 **Memphis, TN** – A significant number of forced entries.
  • 🔹 **Kansas City, MO** – Increasing business vandalism cases.
  • 🔹 **New Orleans, LA** – Notorious for break-ins and looting.
  • 🔹 **Cleveland, OH** – A growing trend in property crimes.
  • 🔹 **Oakland, CA** – High rates of home and business burglaries.
  • 🔹 **Little Rock, AR** – Increasing security concerns in residential areas.
  • 🔹 **Stockton, CA** – Frequently targeted for smash-and-grab crimes.

Weather Protection & Reinforcement

Extreme weather events, including hurricanes, heavy storms, and tornadoes, can cause devastating damage to homes and businesses. Protecting entry points from wind, debris, and pressure changes is critical for minimizing destruction.

🔹 How Weather Damage Affects Structures

  • 🌪️ **High winds** can break windows and doors, leading to interior destruction.
  • 🌊 **Heavy rain and flooding** cause structural weakening and mold buildup.
  • ⚡ **Hail and debris impact** can shatter glass and compromise security.
  • 🔥 **Extreme heat and UV exposure** can weaken window films and frames over time.

Solutions for Weather Protection

🔹 Security & Storm-Resistant Window Film

Specialized window film strengthens glass, reducing shattering from high winds and debris impact.

  • ✅ Holds shattered glass together upon impact.
  • ✅ Helps withstand hurricane-force winds.
  • ✅ Reduces UV exposure that weakens materials.

🔹 Reinforced Door & Window Frames

Reinforcing frames increases resistance to high-speed winds and pressure changes caused by storms.

  • ✅ Prevents frame warping from wind and rain.
  • ✅ Strengthens entry points against forced pressure.
  • ✅ Improves long-term durability in extreme conditions.

🔹 Weather-Resistant Locking Systems

High-security locks designed for extreme weather conditions ensure entry points remain secure.

  • ✅ Corrosion-resistant materials for longevity.
  • ✅ Reinforced deadbolts to prevent storm-related failures.
  • ✅ Works in extreme temperatures and moisture exposure.
